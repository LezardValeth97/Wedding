When We First Met

We first met through mutual friends at a university gathering in 2018. Thanh Thủy was studying literature while Quốc Việt was finishing his degree in computer science. What started as friendly conversations soon blossomed into something more.

After countless coffee dates, weekend trips to Hạ Long Bay, and evening walks along Hồ Tây (West Lake), we knew we had found our perfect match. Two years later, during a special dinner overlooking the city lights of Hà Nội, Quốc Việt proposed.

Now, we're excited to begin our journey together as husband and wife, surrounded by our beloved family and friends. We can't wait to celebrate this special day with all of you!
